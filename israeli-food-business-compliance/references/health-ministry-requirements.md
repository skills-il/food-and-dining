# Ministry of Health Licensing Requirements for Food Businesses

## Overview

All food businesses in Israel require a business license (רישיון עסק) under the Business Licensing Law (חוק רישוי עסקים, התשכ"ח-1968). The Ministry of Health (משרד הבריאות) is the primary approving authority for food-related businesses. Municipal authorities (רשות מקומית) issue the actual license after receiving MOH approval.

## Requirements by Business Type

### Restaurant / Cafe (מסעדה / בית קפה)
- **Required Documents:** Kitchen layout plan (approved by engineer), ventilation plan, fire safety certificate, lease agreement or ownership proof, business registration (ח.פ./ע.מ.)
- **Facility Standards:** Minimum kitchen size per seating capacity, separate handwashing stations (not shared with food prep sinks), hot water supply, tiled walls and floors (washable surfaces), adequate refrigeration and freezer capacity, grease trap installed
- **Inspection Criteria:** Kitchen cleanliness, food storage (off-floor, labeled, dated), temperature compliance (cold < 5°C, hot > 60°C), pest-free environment, staff hygiene (health certificates, clean uniforms, hair covered)
- **Renewal:** Annual. Requires re-inspection if violations found in previous year.

### Food Production / Factory (מפעל מזון)
- **Required Documents:** GMP compliance documentation, production process flowcharts, HACCP plan, lab testing results (microbiological, chemical), water quality reports, waste disposal plan
- **Facility Standards:** Separate zones (raw materials, production, packaging, storage), positive air pressure in clean areas, employee hygiene facilities (changing rooms, showers), loading dock separation, pest control barriers at all entry points
- **Inspection Criteria:** GMP adherence, batch traceability, temperature control through production chain, lab testing schedule compliance, employee training records
- **Renewal:** Annual. Additional unannounced inspections throughout the year.

### Food Truck / Stand (דוכן / רכב מזון)
- **Required Documents:** Mobile food permit, vehicle safety certificate, water supply certification (potable water tank), waste disposal plan, route/location permits from each municipality
- **Facility Standards:** Enclosed food preparation area, potable water tank (minimum 40 liters), wastewater collection tank, handwashing station with soap, adequate refrigeration (battery or generator powered)
- **Inspection Criteria:** Water supply freshness, waste disposal compliance, food temperature during transport and service, vehicle cleanliness
- **Renewal:** Annual. Location permits may need separate renewal per municipality.

### Bakery (מאפייה)
- **Required Documents:** Oven safety certification, flour storage compliance documentation, fire safety certificate, ventilation assessment (flour dust extraction)
- **Facility Standards:** Fire-rated oven installation, flour storage (sealed, off-floor, pest-proof), dedicated ingredient storage, dough preparation area with washable surfaces, adequate ventilation for heat and flour dust
- **Inspection Criteria:** Oven safety compliance, flour storage conditions, allergen separation (gluten cross-contamination controls), temperature logs for proofing and storage
- **Renewal:** Annual. Oven safety re-certification every 2 years.

### Catering (קייטרינג)
- **Required Documents:** Base kitchen license (same as restaurant), transport vehicle permits, temperature monitoring equipment certificates, event-specific permits (for large events 200+ guests)
- **Facility Standards:** Commercial kitchen with full restaurant requirements, insulated transport containers, temperature logging devices, separate vehicles for raw and cooked food (or cleaned between uses)
- **Inspection Criteria:** Transport temperature compliance (cold chain unbroken), time between preparation and service (max 4 hours without temperature control), event site hygiene setup
- **Renewal:** Annual for base kitchen. Event permits per event.

### Market Stall (דוכן שוק)
- **Required Documents:** Municipal market permit, stall assignment documentation, cold storage documentation for perishables
- **Facility Standards:** Covered stall with protection from sun/rain, refrigerated display for perishables, handwashing access, pest control measures, waste disposal arrangement
- **Inspection Criteria:** Product freshness and dating, cold storage temperatures, stall cleanliness, product separation (raw/cooked, meat/dairy)
- **Renewal:** Annual. Municipal market permits may follow different schedules.

## Common Violations and Penalties

| Violation | Severity | Typical Penalty |
|-----------|----------|----------------|
| Operating without a license | Critical | Closure order + fine (up to 226,000 NIS) |
| Temperature abuse (cold food > 5°C) | Critical | Warning + mandatory correction within 24 hours |
| Pest evidence (droppings, live insects) | Critical | Closure until pest-free certification obtained |
| Expired products on premises | Major | Fine + product confiscation |
| Missing staff health certificates | Major | Fine + staff suspended until certificates obtained |
| Inadequate handwashing facilities | Major | Warning + mandatory correction within 7 days |
| Poor cleaning documentation | Minor | Warning + 30 days to implement cleaning schedule |
| Missing temperature logs | Minor | Warning + 14 days to implement logging system |

## Renewal Schedule Summary

| Certificate/License | Renewal Period | Lead Time for Renewal |
|---------------------|---------------|----------------------|
| Business license | Annual | Start 90 days before expiry |
| Kashrut certificate | Annual | Start 60 days before expiry |
| Fire safety certificate | Annual | Start 60 days before expiry |
| Health inspection | Every 6-12 months | Scheduled by MOH |
| Pest control documentation | Quarterly | Ongoing contract |
| Staff health certificates | Per employee, varies | Check monthly for upcoming expirations |
| Vehicle permits (food trucks) | Annual | Start 60 days before expiry |
